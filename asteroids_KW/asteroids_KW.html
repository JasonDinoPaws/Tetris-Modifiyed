Controls(stick with me here this might be a little much):
    • Movement - WASD
    • Fire - Left Click
    • Switch Weapons - 1,2,3 and 4
    • Super - when the green meter is full, Right Click to fire, each weapon has a different super move, currently on the HTML5 version of the game the turret super is broken, when it gets destroyed the game will crash.
