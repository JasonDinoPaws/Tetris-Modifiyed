Tetris Modified

Made by J.P.

A classic recreation of NES Tetris but with some modifications.

Controls

Menu:
    • Left/A, Right/D - Game type selection and level selection
    • Up/W, Down/S - Music type selection and mod selection
    • Enter - Next set

Game Play:
    • Left/A,Right/D - Horizontal movement
    • Down/S - Move down faster
    • Press E to reset all.
